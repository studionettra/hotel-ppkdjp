# RESERVATION_CONFIRMATION_LAYOUT

## Overview

Reservation Confirmation untuk PPKD Hotel dengan layout modern, siap
ditampilkan di web maupun diekspor ke PDF A4.

## Struktur

1.  Header
2.  Reservation Summary
3.  Guest Information
4.  Stay Information
5.  Payment Summary
6.  Payment Method
7.  Hotel Information
8.  Important Information
9.  Footer

## Header

-   Logo Hotel
-   Nama Hotel
-   Reservation Confirmation
-   Booking Number
-   Reservation Date
-   Status Reservasi

## Guest Information

  Field        Keterangan
  ------------ ---------------
  Guest Name   Nama tamu
  Company      Perusahaan
  Email        Email
  Phone        Nomor telepon

## Stay Information

  Field         Keterangan
  ------------- -------------------
  Check In      Tanggal check-in
  Check Out     Tanggal check-out
  Total Night   Lama menginap
  Guest         Jumlah tamu
  Room Type     Tipe kamar
  Room Rate     Tarif per malam

## Payment Summary

-   Room Charge
-   Additional Charge
-   Discount
-   Tax
-   Service Charge
-   **Grand Total**

## Payment Method

### Cash

-   Payment Status
-   Amount

### Bank Transfer

-   Bank Name
-   Account Name
-   Account Number

## Hotel Information

-   Phone
-   Email
-   Website
-   Address

## Important Information

-   Check-in 14.00
-   Check-out 12.00
-   Reservasi tanpa jaminan dilepas pukul 18.00
-   Pembatalan H-1 dikenakan biaya 1 malam.

## Export

-   A4 Portrait
-   Margin 20 mm
-   DomPDF / Browsershot
